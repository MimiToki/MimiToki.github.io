WE ARE CEPPO

1969: The company established 'CEPPO'. I learned a lot of things through 15 years pasta research and worked at that japan in order to develop pasta. I was successful in developing the original menu of CEPPO.

When Roland first opened his pasta stall, he sold neapolitan pasta common in Banshu district in Japan. Banshu district is one of the oldest pasta stores in Kobe, and is considered a progenitor of neapolitan pasta.

A typical type of neapolitan pasta, it serves neapolitan pasta using seafood. The Kobe pasta are obtained by soaking pasta in the wheat seafood broth.
